The “Double Chance” offer

For all the partners that didn’t have the chance to join the travel qualification for Singapore, you have the opportunity to do so in December. Become a Sapphire, stay qualified until May and participate in the voyage.

At the same time, if during September-November you joined qualification and earned QB and NQB, then these points will also count towards qualification. A reminder that the points of December’s Sapphires and higher will be doubled in January. Also, a reminder that your personal points (QB) during the qualification period should total 12.

We get them through the following ranks:

  • Pearl - 1 point
  • Sapphire - 2 points,
  • Ruby - 3 points,
  • Emerald - 4 points,
  • Diamond - 5 points

New qualifying points (NQB) is the total number of points of your first line qualifying partners, when they qualify a certain rank for their first time during the qualifying period using the following calculation:

  • Topaz - 1 point
  • Pearls - 2 points
  • Sapphire - 3 points,
  • Ruby - 4 points
  • Emerald - 5 points
  • Diamond - 6 points

NQB should total 20 points (if two partners are participating, then 25 NQB).

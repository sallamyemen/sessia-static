“DOUBLE YOUR POINTS” offer

In December, reach or confirm your top rank in 2021 (not lower than Sapphire) and double your January points to qualify for the trip to Singapore.

For example, in December you reach your top rank of Ruby and receive 3 qualifying points (QB) and in your first line two partners reach the Pearl rank for the first time, so you get 4 new qualifying points (NQB).

In January, you become a Ruby and your points double, so you receive 6 qualifying points (QB), as for your two partners in the first line, who qualify as Pearls for the first time, you get 8 new qualifying points (NQB).

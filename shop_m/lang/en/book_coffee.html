BOOK “MY COFFEE START-UP”

ABOUT SETS

By Narek Sirakanyan, 2017. 120 pp.

Narek Sirakanyan, who is the President of The Freedom Group and founder of the brand, as well as the author of the book, described it as “the Bible for COFFEECELL partners.”

The book tells how the idea of ​​a new brand was born and carried out in an interesting and informative form of the business plan. In the book you will also find how Freedom Group developed the brand and brought to market the line of health-improving coffee and tea products based on the root of the imperial ginseng, as well as what financial and business model was used to turn this idea into reality and what difficulties had to be overcome. The author concentrates the readers' attention not so much on the health-improving qualities of their products, but on the huge opportunities for financial independence, prosperity and self-development that are open to everyone who wants to enjoy COFFEECELL products and recommend them to other people.

The book will be especially useful to those who are interested in improving the quality of life, and have at least once thought about starting their own business and would like to launch their own coffee start-up.

SHAKER

ABOUT SETS

Volume: 500 ml

The shaker BING HAN is designed to mix ginseng powder with water in a fast and convenient way, and to prepare ginseng drinks. It is made of high-quality durable materials. The shaker can also be used for making various cocktails and carrying different beverages.

Lightweight, ergonomic, with a comfortable handle-strap and modern design, the shaker will be an indispensable accessory for those who would like to have the efficient imperial ginseng ready at hand anywhere: on the road, on a business trip or at work, and it is perfect for those who are leading a healthy lifestyle and are not afraid to show it.

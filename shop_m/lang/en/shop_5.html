About the orgainic Reishi mushroom

Reishi — is a polyporus, which has been famous for its many healing properties for more than 4000 years. It is called "The King of Phytotherapy" and its composition is truly unique - over 150 antioxidants and more than 200 phytonutrients! The reishi mushroom is cultivated in China under special climatic conditions on the wood of fruit trees. Thanks to the rich content of glucans (polysaccharides), triterpenoids, amino acids and organic germanium, the reishi mushroom is known in medicine as a natural antioxidant and immunomodulator and has a wide range of therapeutic applications.

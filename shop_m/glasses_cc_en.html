COFFEECELL CUPS, 100 pcs.

Volume: 200 ml.
COFFEECELL cups have the original multi-colored camouflage design with our logo and slogan. Thanks to the two-layered cup and the double lid, the aroma and heat of the freshly brewed coffee is saved for a very long time.

COFFEECELL cups are excellent promo accessories for coffee-meetings and presentations. These cups can also become indispensable companions for those who like to drink their coffee on the go and choose comfort and style.

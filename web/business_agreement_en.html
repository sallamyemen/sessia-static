  9. DISSEMINATION OF KICKBACKS TO END CUSTOMERS.

    When an End Customer makes a purchase through the Sessia App or platform, Merchant’s cashier first scans the Sessia QR-code (quick response code) of the End Customer from his or her smartphone. On the cashier’s screen, the cashier will see the name of the End Customer. The cashier then scans the QR Code on the check (or takes a picture thereof if the check does not have a QR code) and verifies the correct price before approving the transaction. Once Merchant’s cashier approves the transaction, only the End Customer, and not the Merchant, can take the picture of the receipt (whether paid by check, card, or cryptocurrency) and check (if applicable, with account information redacted), which Sessia publishes instantly and automatically on Sessia’s internal feed (the “Sessia Social Feed”) where the End Customer’s friends, contacts, and other Sessia users can see it. The End Customer (and not the Merchant) may thereafter also post a picture and other information of the product purchased. The End Customer’s friends, followers, and other Sessia’s users can comment, like, or share the post on other social media platforms to generate referrals to that Merchant and kickbacks to the original End Customer.

    • Upon each transaction the End Customer makes through Sessia, the End Customer will automatically receive Merchant’s available Cashbacks in the form of kicks. In addition, the End Customer will automatically receive kickbacks disbursed from Merchant’s Sessia Wallet but only after the End Customer’s followers and friends make purchases from the same Merchant subsequent to seeing and responding to the End Customer’s purchase on the Sessia Social Feed.
    • As a condition precedent to a Merchant's issuance of any kickbacks: (i) the End Customer must make an actual purchase from that Merchant through Sessia; (ii) and Sessia must confirm that the follower or user has seen the End Customer's transaction receipt or check published on the "Sessia Social Feed". Sessia recognizes who has seen an End Customer’s post by his/her/its followers or friends engaging in one of the following tasks: (i) clicking a “like”; (iii) clicking on the check Icon on the right hand side of the banner; (iii) writing or posting comments; or (iv) “flipping the banners. Any of these four tasks enables Sessia to confirm that the friend, follower, or other user is transacting with Merchant in response to the original End Customer’s purchase from that Merchant. It is at that point only, that Merchant is obligated to reward the kickbacks to the End Customer.
    • Effect of Refund Policies and Erroneous Merchant Charges - End Customers will not receive their kickbacks until the expiration of the period a customer or client is eligible for a refund of any returned good, product, or merchandise pursuant to Merchant’s Return/Refund policy as disclosed on Merchant’s Online Store, as set forth in Paragraph 12 and Paragraph 14 below. On the other hand, in the event, Merchant erroneously overcharges an End Customer that results in Merchant’s issuance of excessive kicks, Sessia will not be able to reverse the transaction, and Merchant is hereby responsible for indemnifying, defending, and holding harmless Sessia, and all of its beneficiaries and officials, for all for any claims, causes of action, or liability between the Merchant and End User arising from any such overcharge or over-issuance of kickbacks.
    • Sessia has the right to display and maintain a rating system for each End customer, based on their purchases and activity in the application. Sessia is entitled at its discretion to create, change and delete a rating system and methodologies using which Sessia rates each End customer compared to other End Customers on the Sessia platform and appoint increasing or decreasing coefficient for each Final client applied to the cashback or kickback base rate depending on the rating of the End customer. The Merchant agrees to pay cashbacks and kickbacks taking into account the rating and not to dispute the results of ratings, including that in court.
    • The User has the right to request cashback payment from the Merchant via the Sessia platform. To do this, the User using the cashback request function indicates the name of the Merchant and uploads to the platform a receipt of the completed purchase from the Merchant. Identification of the ownership of the check is determined by the TIN, indicated on the check. The merchant must consider the application within 14 (fourteen) calendar days, approve or refuse it, indicating reason. At the time of approval of the application, the requested amount will be debited from the Merchant’s Wallet and credited to the User’s Wallet. The User whose request was rejected has the right to re-request cashback by providing additional proof of purchase.

    • End Customer #1 make a purchase at a global chain coffee merchant. End Customer # 2 sees an electronic copy the check or receipt of that purchase (redacted for personal identifiable information) reflecting that End Customer #1 made a purchase at that Merchant.

Home Party

Become the host of your personal talkshow and introduce our products and brands during a live stream on VKontakte and Instagram!

The Home Party is an interactive gastronomic show that features restaurant chefs and COFFEECELL partners. The host chooses a restaurant and menu, invites guests and the company covers the costs of the event.

To become the host, you need to successively complete the following statuses as part of the Freedom International Group privilege program:

  1. 10 000 points = Silver
  2. 50 000 points = Gold
  3. 100 000 points = Platinum
  4. 10 000 points = Ambassador's team
  5. 15 000 points = Home Party

Having fulfilled these conditions, you will be able to hold a Home Party in your city within a month.

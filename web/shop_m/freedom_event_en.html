Freedom Event: rules of conduct


Basic provisions

As part of the Freedom Event (FE) status, a partner who has collected the required number of points under the FIG privilege program receives the right to host their own offline event at the company’s expense.

FE purpose and objectives

Freedom Event is a massive event to promote products and brands that are part of FIG and attract new customers and partners.

The hosted Freedom Event may be considered successful if at the event:

  1. There were at least 25 newbies registered on the PV/CC app.
  2. Newcomers placed at least 5 product orders for 475 CV (Project V) or 50,000 CV (COFFEECELL).

Format

The Freedom Event is held in a presentation format using all the necessary technical and other means of audio and video support (screens, projectors, microphones, flipcharts, etc.).

Also, within the framework of the event, the following accompanying procedures are encouraged:

  • Tasting of company products (coffee, tea, etc.)
  • Demonstration of cosmetic procedures with our products
  • Testing using equipment and instruments that were developed in-house
  • Giveaways, competitions, and quizzes promoting our products and brands
  • Performances by artists, live music, DJ sets
  • Professional video and photography, including the organization of a photo zone, decorated in the style of our brands and company

Not allowed during the FE:

  • The consumption of alcoholic beverages, except in exceptional cases approved by the company
  • Demonstrations and promotion of third-party products and services
  • Use of branded banners, press walls, roll-ups, presentations, and other marketing materials not developed by FIG’s designers or not approved by the company in advance
  • Using unbranded disposable tableware for tastings and treating event guests

Event venue

A business-level conference room with at least 70 people capacity, equipped with a stage, screen and all the necessary modern multimedia equipment.

Location options:

  • Hotels with at least 4*
  • Prestigious business center

Guest seating options:

  • Audience hall
  • Separate tables (6-8 people)

No standing options are allowed for guests!

FE are not held:

  • In restaurants, cafes, bars, clubs and entertainment venues
  • In the classrooms of universities, colleges, and other educational centers

FE organization procedure

The organizing partner:

  • Selects a hall (venue) and approves it with the company
  • Negotiates with the hall administration
  • Draws up an event program and approves it with the company
  • Approves the cost estimates with the company
  • Invites speakers and guests
  • Organizes and conducts a campaign to attract participants in their city
  • Prepares handout materials
  • Conducts the event as a presenter (optional)
  • Organizes photo and video shooting
  • Provides a report about the event, as well as photo and video materials

The FIG company:

  • Approves the program and budget presented by the partner
  • Pays for the costs of the event
  • At the partner’s request, posts event announcements in the application’s feed and calendar
  • Provides products for tasting within the approved budget
  • Publishes a post-promotion post in the application feed about the FE held using photo and video materials from the event

Dates of the event

The Freedom Event is held no later than the next calendar month after the partner achieves their status.

In case of delay, the opportunity to hold a Freedom Event at the company’s expense is canceled, and points written off for this status are not returned.

FE Payment Terms

The company allocates funds to host the Freedom Event:

  • Up to $1,500 - if 30 or more newcomers not previously registered in the application participated in the event.
  • No more than $1200 - if there were less than 30 newcomers.

All costs over the approved estimate are to be paid independently by the partner.

Final Provisions

Holding events of this level will not only raise your authority in our community and emphasize your top status but will also help attract new participants and motivate your team.

The Company reserves the right to refuse payment for a Freedom Event if the event's program, venue, and organization do not meet our corporate standards and may harm the reputation of FIG and its brands.

The Project V Privilege Program

The Project V Privilege Program is an efficient and simple bonus accrual system developed by Sessia software. The company's deductions for the payment of bonuses amount to 56% of the turnover, not counting special bonuses and promotions. In addition, partners have the opportunity to purchase products with a 40% discount and sell them to the consumers, thereby increasing their income.

The Project V Privilege Program is a balanced payout system based on the following types of income generation:

  1. Retail bonus
  2. Referral bonus
  3. Partner cashback
  4. Partner bonus
  5. Kickback
  6. Leader bonus
  7. StarMaker bonus
  8. Jump bonus

In addition to Project V Privilege Program payments, there are special incentive programs:

  1. Booster

In order to explain the Project V Privilege Program, let us explain some of the definitions that we will see later.

DEFINITIONS

CV (Commission Volume):

CV - a special unit of trade. All terms of the Privilege Program are expressed in CV terms.

Each product has its own number of points, the CV base. The CV base of products is the same in all countries, which allows for the unification of the Project V Privilege Program and to do international business in any country open to Project V with a unified Privilege Program for all countries (for the time being, business is being done in 17 countries).

Each product’s CV base is indicated in the Project V store on the Sessia mobile application for each product.

The CV base serves as the basis for accruing the Company’s partner bonuses.

Personal Volume (PV) is the sum of CV orders placed per month by the partner themself and/or their clients who placed an order using his Partner Code through the Company's online store https://projectvint.ru. or the Sessia mobile application.

Personal Group Volume (PGV) is the sum of the Personal Volumes (including their own PV) of partners in the partner organization, excluding the personal volumes of partners with the rank of Senior Partner and their organizations (see the description of partner ranks below).

SPONSORSHIP

A sponsor is a partner who invites other partners to their organization. Your sponsor is the person who invited you to Project V.
For partners invited by you or your partners, you become a sponsor, and they form your organization.

Partners that you personally invited are your first line (1st level). In turn, the partners who were invited by the partners of your first line are your second line (2nd level), and so on. Organizations that start with your first line partners are Branches of your organization.
The partner who invited you is your first level sponsor. In turn, the partner who invited your sponsor is your second level sponsor, and so on. All your sponsors shape your sponsorship vertical.

DYNAMIC COMPRESSION

Dynamic compression is the principle of calculating partner rewards and qualifications.
To calculate the qualifications for the Project V Career Plan and partner remuneration for blocks 2-3, a Compression Tree is built.
To do so:

  1. All partners with the rank of Senior Partner are determined.
  2. In the Compression Tree, the PV of partners with a rank below the Senior Partner are included in the PGV of the Senior Partner (the PGV of each Senior Partner is calculated this way).
  3. The Senior Partner (with their PGV) is "pulled up" to the higher sponsor - the Senior Partner (i.e. is placed at their 1st level in the Compression Tree).

Based on the resulting Compression Tree, levels and qualifications are calculated, with partner rewards calculated in accordance with the percentages and levels of a particular bonus.

In order to explain the Coffeecell Privilege Program, let's explain some definitions that we will come across hereinafter.

DEFINITIONS

CV (Commission Volume)
CV - is a special unit of trade. All terms of the Privilege Program are expressed in CV terms.
Each product has its own number of points, the CV base. The products’ CV base is the same in all countries, which allows us to unify the Coffeecell Privilege Program and to conduct international business in any open Coffeecell country with a single Privilege Program for all countries (currently operating in 17 countries).
Each product’s CV base is indicated in the Coffeecell store on the Sessia mobile application for each product.

The CV base serves as the base for the accrual of the Company’s partner bonuses Personal Volume (PV) is the sum of CV orders made per month by the partner and/or their clients who placed an order using their Partner Code through the Company's online store https://coffeecell.com/ or in the Sessia mobile application.
Booster CVs will be credited to your gift account and you can use them to purchase Coffeecell products. You can choose a gift for the accumulated amount when placing your next order. At the same time, it is not obligatory to immediately use the gift CVs; you can continue to accumulate them in your gift account.

SPONSORSHIP

A sponsor is a partner who invites other partners into their organization. Your sponsor is the person that invited you to Coffeecell.
You become the sponsor to the partners whom you or your partners invite and they form your organization.
The partners that you personally invited are your first line (1st level). In turn, the partners who were invited by the partners of your first line are your second line (2nd level), and so on.
Organizations that are started by the partners of your first line are Branches of your organization.
The partner who invited you is your first level sponsor. In turn, the partner who invited your sponsor is your second level sponsor, and so on. All of your sponsors make up your Sponsor Vertical.

Business case is a set of Coffeecell products that must be purchased in order to become a member of the Privilege Program.
The minimal price of acquired Coffeecell products to participate in the Privilege Program is 6 000 CV.
Activity is the minimum monthly amount of acquired Coffeecell products that is required to remain a member of the Privilege Program.
To confirm Activity is to make monthly purchases of Coffeecell products on the https://coffeecell.com/ website or in the Sessia application. The minimal amount of purchases required to confirm activity is 6 000 CV each month.

  1. Partner discount

    When you buy any Coffeecell products as a partner of the Company, you get a 20% discount.

  2. Partner cashback

    If your partners, who are first line partners in your organization and have not yet purchased a Business Case, purchase Coffeecell products, you will receive 20% from each of their purchases.

  3. Kickback

    After obtaining the Business Case, you begin to earn a percentage on the purchases of your partners in all four lines of your organization, who themselves have a Business Case. This means that you accrue Kickback from each purchase that your partner makes.
    The size of your Kickback depends on the line of the partner that makes the purchase.

    Partner’s line Kickback size
    First line 10 %
    Second line 5%
    Third line 5%

    Cashback and Kickback accrual and use:
    Cashback and Kickback are accrued based on the cost of the orders in the national currency and are credited to a special individual internal account at the end of the month. Funds from this account can be used to pay for orders in subsequent months.

  4. Booster

    When placing personal orders in the amount of 15 000 CVs or more during a month, a partner can receive 1 500 gift CVs. With an increase in the amount of the order, the number of gift CVs increases: for every additional 5 000 CVs spent on the purchase of Coffeecell products, the partner receives an additional 500 gift CVs.
    This amount is offset by all orders made during the given month, including the current one. I.e., as soon as the partner's amount of personal orders (PV), including the current one, reaches 15 000 CVs, they immediately receive 1 500 CV. They can be spent immediately or later.
    All unspent CVs are accumulated in the "Gift CV" account and the partner can use them later.

  5. Topaz +

    When placing personal orders in the amount of 50 000 CVs or more within a month, a partner can receive 5 000 gift CVs. With an increase in the amount of the order, the number of gift CVs increases: for every additional 5 000 CVs spent on the purchase of Coffeecell products, the partner receives an additional 500 gift CVs.
    Further, in each of the following 6 months, the partner has the opportunity to receive an additional 1 500 CV, subject to the following conditions: the partner has purchased a Business Case and confirms their Activity monthly.
    All unspent CVs are accumulated in the "Gift CV" account and the partner can use them later.

  6. Business Cashback

    Business Cashback is a fixed amount that is credited to you every month when your Organization reaches a certain turnover and performs personal Activity. The amount of Business Cashback depends on your rank (see Coffeecell Career Plan below for more details) and can reach 100 000 USD.

You will see the tightening, regenerating, protective, moisturizing and antiaging effect of SOS Hydrating Ampoules from the first day of application.
When used daily, this luxurious fluid helps restore your skin at the cellular level, giving it a young, radiant, and rested look, and giving your skin a feeling of tenderness, freshness, and vitality.

The product is designed and produced in Switzerland.

    Helps to shrink enlarged pores, improve uneven skin tone, reduce dullness and wrinkles, effectively smoothes the surface of the skin and brightens age spots.

    Stimulates cell regeneration and collagen production, contributes to the rapid recovery of the skin and gives it elasticity.

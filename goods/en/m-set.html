Main ingredients:
bakuchiol
psoralea hazel-leaf seed extract helps to reduce the depth of wrinkles and prevent the appearance of new ones, stimulates the production of collagen in the skin. it has antioxidant properties and helps to preserve the youthfulness of skin, providing an anti-age effect. shows antibacterial activity in the fight against acne and inflammation on the face. suppresses the production of melanin, helping to reduce the level of pigmentation, including age-related. unlike retinol, which functions similarly, bakuchiol does not cause irritation and does not increase the sensitivity of the skin to the sun and is therefore suitable for sensitive skin and can be used at any time of the year.

project v complex
it contains vegetable glycerin, fig extract, ginkgo biloba, ginseng. thanks to the combination of these active ingredients, the complex has a lifting effect, helps the skin regain its tone, elasticity and even out the relief, as well as protect it from photodamage. providing exfoliating action, the complex stimulates intensive cell renewal, thereby slowing down the aging process of the skin.

Là S. Hyaluronic Serum
The Là S ultra-moisturizing hyaluronic serum contains a highly concentrated active complex of high molecular and low molecular hyaluronic acid, which leads to the comprehensive transformation of the skin at the cellular level, fights dryness and skin dehydration.
The serum eliminates the signs of age-related changes, filling the skin with moisture and ensuring more enhanced production of collagen. The result is skin that is more elastic, smooth and charged with energy.
It instantly soothes and relieves irritation after solar burns, aggressive peeling, and cleaning. Helps maintain the result of injections and other cosmetic and surgical procedures.
The active ingredients of the serum are developed and produced in Switzerland.
Properties:
  • Instantly removes dryness and the appearance of age-related changes
  • Evens out skin tone, eliminating pigmentation and other imperfections
  • Promotes the production of collagen, increasing skin tone
  • Long-lasting hydration from the first use
  • Fights peeling, filling the skin with moisture
  • Soothes the skin
  • Makes the face radiant and healthy
  • Renews skin in 30 days
Main ingredients:
  • Hyaluronic acid
    Hyaluronic acid with low molecular weight consists of molecules with a size of 50 kDa and deeply penetrates the skin. It retains water in the connective tissue, makes the skin more elastic, noticeably reduces wrinkles, and has a longer-lasting effect.
  • Allantoin
    Stimulates the natural process of skin rejuvenation. It improves the processes of regeneration and regular cell renewal. Helps to get rid of acne and even out skin tone.
  • Panthenol
    Has a soothing and softening effect. Has an antioxidant effect, regenerates skin cells, lifting and moisturizing it. Holds moisture over the surface of the skin, making the skin look elastic and nourished.
  • Niacinamide
    It helps narrow enlarged pores, evens out tone, fights pigmentation, transforms the dull color of the face and weakened turgor, making the skin elastic and radiant. Reduces the impact of negative environmental factors on the skin.
  • Ectoin
    It is a multifunctional active ingredient that fights the consequences of stress, causing cell damage. Restores the natural barrier of the skin, preventing UV-aging. Replenishes the natural supply of moisture, keeping it in the skin for up to 7 days. Eliminates peeling and irritation.
Ingredients:
Aqua (Swiss Glacial Water), Sodium Hyaluronate, Propylene Glycol, Glycerin, Phenoxyethanol, Chlorphenesin, Lavandula Angustifolia Flower Water, Citrus Aurantium Amara Flower Water, Benzoic Acid, Allantoin, Niacinamide, Ectoin, Disodium EDTA, PEG-40 Hydrogenated Castor Oil.
Recommendations:
Apply a few drops of serum to the cleansed skin of the face, neck and décolleté. Distribute it using light tapping movements along the massage lines. The product is recommended for daily use, in the morning and in the evening. It can be used regularly or as a course, in combination with Là J cream and Là P ampoules. For external use only.
Product form:
30 ml.
Storage:
Store at a temperature between 5°C and 25°C in a dry place, out of the reach of children.
